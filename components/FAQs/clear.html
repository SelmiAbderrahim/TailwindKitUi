What am I seeing, hearing, feeling, smelling & tasting?

Nature is first and foremost a sensory experience, but many people are too caught up in their thoughts to notice. This first question will help you focus on what you’re seeing, hearing and feeling in the world around you. It’s really very simple to get out of your head and wake up to what’s happening around you.

Where am I feeling drawn to explore?

One of the BIG secrets to accelerated nature discovery is to follow your curiosity. Curiosity is like an endless source of renewable energy that will activate your naturalist mind and help you to recall your outdoor experiences with vivid clarity. How does this work on a practical level?
